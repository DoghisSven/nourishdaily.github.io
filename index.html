  • 10-Minute Avocado Toast
    Show Recipe ▼

    Ingredients:

    • 1 ripe avocado
    • 2 slices of whole-grain bread
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Optional: lemon juice, red pepper flakes

    Instructions:

    1. Toast the bread to your desired crispness.

    2. Mash the avocado with a fork until smooth.

    3. Drizzle with olive oil, and add salt and pepper.

    4. Spread the avocado mixture over the toasted bread. Optionally, add a squeeze of lemon juice and sprinkle with red pepper flakes for an extra flavor kick.

    Perfect for a quick breakfast or a healthy snack!

  • Refreshing Green Smoothie
    Show Recipe ▼

    Ingredients:

    • 1 banana
    • 1 cup fresh spinach
    • 1/2 cup almond milk (or other milk of choice)
    • 1 tbsp chia seeds
    • 1/2 cup ice (optional)

    Instructions:

    1. Place all ingredients into a blender.

    2. Blend on high until smooth and creamy.

    3. Serve immediately for a refreshing, nutrient-packed start to your day!
